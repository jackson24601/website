The method

Simple, repeatable coaching that builds skill every session.

1

Diagnose

Clarify goals, audience, event rules, judging criteria, and the student's current blockers.

Common coaching goals

Support that feels encouraging and concrete.

Build a clear outline, opening, transitions, and rehearsal checklist.

Refine delivery choices, pacing, emphasis, gestures, and judge impact.
